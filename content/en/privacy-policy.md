### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We're talking Cookies and similar tracking tech! We use 'em to keep an eye on activity on our Service and stash away certain info. The tracking tech we roll with includes beacons, tags, and scripts, all for collecting and tracking info, plus boosting and analyzing our Service. The tech we might use includes:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a small file dropped onto your Device. You can tell your browser to totally refuse all Cookies or to give you a heads-up when one's being sent. But heads-up: if you don't play nice with Cookies, you might miss out on some parts of our Service. Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to reject Cookies, our Service might just go ahead and use 'em anyway.
- **Web Beacons**: Some parts of our Service and our emails might have these tiny electronic files called Web Beacons (also known as clear GIFs, pixel tags, and single-pixel GIFs). These bad boys let the Company, for example, count users who've visited those pages or opened an email, and they're also used for other related website stats (like tracking how popular a certain section is and making sure systems and servers are all good).

Cookies can be either "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Think of Persistent Cookies as the ones that stick around on your personal computer or mobile device even when you're offline, while Session Cookies vanish into thin air as soon as you close your web browser. Poof!

We actually use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the reasons we're about to lay out below. Get ready!

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are absolutely crucial for hooking you up with services available through the Website and letting you use some of its cool features. Without these Cookies, the services you're asking for just wouldn't be possible, and we only use them to make sure you get those services. No kidding!

  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are all about remembering if you've given the thumbs-up to using Cookies on the Website. So we don't nag you every time!

- **Functional Cookies**

  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are super handy because they let us remember the choices you make when you're cruising through the Website, like recalling your login deets or language preferences. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized experience and save you the hassle of re-entering your preferences every single time you visit. Pretty neat, huh?
